2 
3 ## Arduino IDE
4 
5 Please install the latest version of the Arduino IDE from <http://arduino.cc>.
6 This tutorial was created & tested with version 1.6.4, but it should work with all newer versions.
7 
8 ## BTstack Library
9 
10 To install the BTstack library, please download it from <http://bluekitchen-gmbh.com/files/btstack-arduino-latest.zip>
11 and unpack it into your Arduino libraries folder. On OS X, that's:
12 
13  ~/Documents/Arduino/libraries
14 
15 After installing the BTstack Library, you have to restart the Arduino IDE to make it show up in the examples section.
16 
17 
18 ## Hardware
19 Please plug the BTstack LE Shield into one of the supported Arduino boards listed on the [Welcome page](../).

24 ## Test
25 
26 As a first test, open the iBeaconScanner example via File->Examples->BTstack->iBeaconScanner.
27 In the Sketch window, press the Upload button.
28 
29 ![Image of Arduino IDE Sketch](picts/sketch.jpg)
30 
31 After it was successfully uploaded to the board,
32 open the Serial Console.
33 
34 The output should look similar to this:

47 This example listens for BLE Advertisements and prints the Bluetooth BD_ADDR (similar to a WiFi MAC) address and the received signal strength. For iBeacons, also the Major ID, the Minor ID and the iBeacon UUID are shown.
