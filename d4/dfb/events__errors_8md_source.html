2 ## L2CAP Events {#sec:eventsAndErrorsAppendix}
3 
4 L2CAP events and data packets are delivered to the packet handler
5 specified by *l2cap_register_service* resp.
6 *l2cap_create_channel_internal*. Data packets have the
7 L2CAP_DATA_PACKET packet type. L2CAP provides the following events:
8 
9 - L2CAP_EVENT_CHANNEL_OPENED - sent if channel establishment is
10  done. Status not equal zero indicates an error. Possible errors: out
11  of memory; connection terminated by local host, when the connection
12  to remote device fails.
13 
14 - L2CAP_EVENT_CHANNEL_CLOSED - emitted when channel is closed. No
15  status information is provided.
16 
17 - L2CAP_EVENT_INCOMING_CONNECTION - received when the connection is
18  requested by remote. Connection accept and decline are performed
19  with *l2cap_accept_connection_internal* and
20  *l2cap_decline_connecti-on_internal* respectively.
21 
22 - L2CAP_EVENT_CREDITS - emitted when there is a chance to send a new
23  L2CAP packet. BTstack does not buffer packets. Instead, it requires
24  the application to retry sending if BTstack cannot deliver a packet
25  to the Bluetooth module. In this case, the l2cap_send_internal
26  will return an error.
27 
28 - L2CAP_EVENT_SERVICE_REGISTERED - Status not equal zero indicates
29  an error. Possible errors: service is already registered;
30  MAX_NO_L2CAP_SERVICES (defined in config.h) already registered.
31

58 ## RFCOMM Events
59 
60 All RFCOMM events and data packets are currently delivered to the packet
61 handler specified by *rfcomm_register_packet_handler*. Data packets
62 have the _DATA_PACKET packet type. Here is the list of events provided
63 by RFCOMM:
64 
65 - RFCOMM_EVENT_INCOMING_CONNECTION - received when the connection
66  is requested by remote. Connection accept and decline are performed
67  with *rfcomm_accept_connection_internal* and
68  *rfcomm_decline_con-nection_internal* respectively.
69 
70 - RFCOMM_EVENT_CHANNEL_CLOSED - emitted when channel is closed. No
71  status information is provided.
72 
73 - RFCOMM_EVENT_OPEN_CHANNEL_COMPLETE - sent if channel
74  establishment is done. Status not equal zero indicates an error.
75  Possible errors: an L2CAP error, out of memory.
76 
77 - RFCOMM_EVENT_CREDITS - The application can resume sending when
78  this even is received. See Section on [RFCOMM credit-based flow-control](../protocols/#sec:flowControlProtocols)
79  for more.
80 
81 - RFCOMM_EVENT_SERVICE_REGISTERED - Status not equal zero indicates
82  an error. Possible errors:
83 
84  - service is already registered;
85  - MAX_NO_RFCOMM_SERVICES (defined in config.h) already registered.
